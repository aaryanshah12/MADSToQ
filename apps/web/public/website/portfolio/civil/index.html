The Experience

Wake to birdsong, not traffic. Live inside the green.

Aranya Grove is planned around what you feel outdoors first — dew on the lawn, wind through trees, the stillness of a lake at dawn. Weekends begin with a walk on a garden trail, not a drive through town. The estate sits near Kadi with easy access from Ahmedabad, yet reads like a private forest edge.

Villas are set low between existing tree lines, with courtyards and verandas that pull the landscape in. Water bodies, thematic gardens, and quiet internal roads do the heavy lifting — your home is a room inside a much larger natural address.

The estate is the experience. Your villa sits inside it.

The Space

Homes drawn around courtyards — with the estate green always in view.

Plans keep tree lines, garden edges, and water within sight from every main room. Private courtyards and verandas frame native planting; passages are short so you step outside often. Architecture stays quiet so the landscape stays loud — in the best way.

Every parcel is landscape-ready, with estate teams supporting planting, lawn care, and shared green zones. Choose a ready villa plan or reserve a larger plot to shape your own garden and court.
