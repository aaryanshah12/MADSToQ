Personal Sales Portal — User manual

Leads, quotations, purchase orders, and expenses for sales teams.

Inventory Inward-Outward PMC Personal Sales

1. Sign in

  1. Go to /personal/sales/login.
  2. Access is granted per organization — contact your admin if sign-in fails.
  3. After login, your organization name appears in the sidebar.

2. Dashboard

Pipeline overview: open leads, quotations awaiting action, and recent activity. Use notification bell for document events.

3. Leads

  • Create leads with company and contact details.
  • Open a lead to update stage, notes, and linked documents.
  • Convert qualified leads into quotations from the lead detail view.

4. Quotations

  • Build rich-text quotations with line items and terms.
  • Preview or send PDF versions to customers when email is configured.
  • Track status from draft through accepted or declined.

5. Purchase orders

  • Generate POs from accepted quotations or manually.
  • Edit line items, delivery terms, and attachments on the PO detail page.

6. Expenses

  • Log travel and operational expenses tied to your sales work.
  • Filter and review history for reimbursement reporting.

7. Tips

  • Save quotation drafts frequently — the editor supports tables and links.
  • Confirm customer email before using Send document.
  • Open User manual from the sidebar footer anytime.
